Another Color, Inc. is a full service visual communications firm that has been in business for over 40 years helping create exciting design solutions for our clients. Experience, insight, and a deep understanding of usability and user behavior allow us to develop and produce solutions that work.

Teamwork best describes the process ACI brings to a communication project. Our constant goal is to communicate with clarity, precision, and visual appeal while giving each client complete and personal attention. The key is developing a coherent strategy by tapping into the market intelligence of the client, understanding the client’s customer, and mapping a strategy with the right medium to reach the customer in an efficient and cost-effective way.

We work as your creative partner, monitoring costs, schedules, and production with the ultimate goal of enhancing and promoting bottom line results from beginning to end. Let us create a new picture for your business.


                           

VISUAL & USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN




Allowing people to experience your product is the best way to communicate the idea behind it. A functional prototype of your app or website helps you do that in a matter of weeks, not months. It's the quickest way to collect valuable feedback on user experience and interface design, learn what features to prioritize in order to get the most return on your early investment, and show investors and corporate partners that you are a serious player. Taking a lean approach, we can work with you all the way from concept and MVP to building out the user experience and product details:


1. STRATEGY

  • understanding the product
  • competitive research
  • user stories creation
  • brand outline

2. DESIGN

  • user journeys
  • low-fidelity interface design
  • UI styles

3. PROTOTYPE

  • complete UI style guide
  • high-fidelity interface design
  • key interaction areas
